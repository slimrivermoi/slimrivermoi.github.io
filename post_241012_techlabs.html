My TechLabs Experience

Completing my first Machine Learning project.

Posted by Coder Cat with ❤️ on Oct 12, 2024

After completing half of my Python course online, I decided to join TechLabs' Digital Shaper Program to put my learning to test. The program provides learning platform for motivated individuals who are interested to deepen their tech knowledge in Web Dev, UX, Data Science or AI areas. I am particularly interested to understand more about ML hence I joined the Data Science track to learn more aspects of data analysis and manipulation with Python and applied ML topics.

My team project was to analyze and create a movie recommendation systems with multiple approaches to illustrate the possibilities and complexities behind such work. You can read more about the summary or visit our work in Github.

The 5-month learning and project journey had been a great learning for me. I enjoyed the process of playing the detective role to dig deeper and finding patterns and connections between the dataset. I also enjoyed brainstorming with my teammates to share our findings and combining our solutions together. Here are a few important techniques I used in this project:

  • Using ast to parse JSON data into Python List.
  • Applying the concept of 'Bag-of-words' to create text based variables.
  • Try simple methods first. ML and AI solutions can consume a lot of resources, sometimes what you need might be a few simple lines of codes to filter the items for results and that works too!
  • Write down a few hypothesis and think of the appropriate ML methods you can apply to test it out.
  • Experiment and try new ways: I am fortunate to have great teammates who are also eager to learn and collaborate. We collaborated and experimented a lot during the project phase and we continue to keep in touch after the project.
  • Network and get to know one others in the program. Everyone has different stories on why they are participating in Techlabs. I find it invigorating to get to know their backgrounds and their approaches in learning.
